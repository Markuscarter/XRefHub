Privacy Policy for Xrefhub

Effective Date: July 30, 2025

Xrefhub is a Chrome extension designed to assist users in reviewing advertisements on the X (formerly Twitter) platform. This extension does not collect or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) from its users.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
  • Authentication credentials or financial data
  • Medical or health-related information
  • Personal messages or communications

Information Accessed

Xrefhub may temporarily access the content of the current browser tab on specific domains (e.g., ads.twitter.com) in order to extract and analyze ad data. This content is processed locally in the user’s browser and is not transmitted externally.

Local Storage

We use Chrome’s local storage API to store user preferences and UI states. This information stays on your device and is not shared.

Data Sharing and Sales

Xrefhub does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties for any purpose.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the extension implies agreement with the current version of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@xrefhub.com.
